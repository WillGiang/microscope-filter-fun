    Zeiss Observer A1

    Winship Clinic C Room 5045

    Features

    The Zeiss Observer A1 is designed for multichannel fluorescence, brightfield, and phase contrast. This microscope has a color camera and is therefore appropriate for histological stains. Excitation is with an X-Cite Series 120Q.

    Objectives:

    • 5X    0.13 NA    N-Achroplan Ph0
    • 10X  0.25 NA    N-Achroplan Ph1
    • 20X  0.40 NA    LDPlan-Neofluar Corr Ph2
    • 20X  0.50 NA    EC Plan-Neofluar
    • 40X  0.75 NA    EC Plan-Neofluar

    Filters:

    Fluorophore Excitation Dichroic Emission
    DAPI BP 335-383 BS 395 BP 420-470
    CFP BP 426-446 BS 455 BP 460-500
    GFP BP 450-490 BS 495 BP 500-550
    Rhodamine BP 540-552 BS 560 BP 575-640
    Cy5 BP 625-655 BS 660 BP 665-715

    Camera:

    ZEISS AxioCam 305, a fast 5 megapixel CMOS color camera


    Acquisition Software:

     Zen PRO
